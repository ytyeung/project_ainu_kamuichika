## Isepo yaieyukar, “Sampaya terke”   
## 兎が自ら歌った謡「サンパヤ テレケ」 
## The Hare's Self Chant “Sampaya terke”   

Sanpaya Tereke

Leaping over two valleys, leaping over three valleys,
Playing along the way, I followed my elder brother to mountains.
Days by days, I followed my elder brother.
When we saw a crossbow trap set up by human,
My elder brother would break it.
I was always laughing at this.
